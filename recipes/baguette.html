Baguette jambon-beurre

Translation: Baguette jambon-beurre

One piece of bread with ham and butter please. No, nothing else, thanks.

Ingredients

  • The best baguette that money can buy
  • The best, highest butter-fat content butter that money can buy
  • The best sliced, cooked ham that money can buy
  • The best flaky salt that money can buy

That is all.

Steps

  1. Cut baguette. Put butter on both sides
  2. Pile on ham
  3. Sprinkle on salt
  4. bon apetit
