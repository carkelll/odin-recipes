bill o' rights chili

10 ingredients chili that even rats would love

yo this cat chli is soooo delicious, you've gotta try it

Ingredients

  • many many cats
  • beans
  • tomate paste
  • one single red chili flake

yea i know i promised 10 ingredients in the chili, but don't get greedy

Steps

  1. get some tomato paste cooking in the pan until golden brown
  2. throw in the kitty cats and beans and sautee until golden brown
  3. prep your chili flake - CAREFUL - VERY SPICY. you can also just dip it in the chili for a second if you are worreid about the spice ^^
  4. cook until golden brown
  5. bon apetit
