Bill o' Rights Chili

10 ingredients that'll make you amend your constitution

Hurry up there pardner this chili won't make itself

Ingredients

  • O&G (hold your horses, prospector - the other O&G)
  • Tomato paste
  • Chopped tomatoes
  • Green peppers
  • Sweet potato
  • Beans of your choice (but kidney beans mandatory)
  • Minced beef (if you swing that way)
  • Spices:
    • Chili poweder
    • Cumin
    • Cayenne
    • Paprika
  • Suggested secret ingredients:
    • Beer
    • Smoked chipotle in adobo
    • Cacao powder
    • Molasses

Yes, I know I promised 10 ingredients in the chili, but don't get greedy

Steps

  1. Dump your mince in and let it get a nice colour on it
  2. Add O&G and quick cook, then throw in tomatoes and tomato puree. Put in peppers at last sec with some of spices
  3. Dump in all the beans & bean liquid. Can throw in some stock if you want it more liquidy
  4. Get it all nice and bubbling then put in rest of spices and your secret ingredients
  5. Top with generous serving of Fritos probably, volumetrically, 2 Fritos: 1 Chili; shredded cheese, fresh cut onions, jalapenos, avocado, sour cream,
  6. bon apetit
