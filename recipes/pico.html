Pico de Gringo

It's like pico de gallo but its for white people who are scared of spicy

You are actually allowed to make this as spicy as you wish.

Ingredients

  • Juice of half a lime or the whole lime probably
  • Half a red onion or the whole onion probably
  • Copious amount of coriander
  • Tomatoes
  • Jalapenos

Let's pico this gallo.

Steps

  1. Chop it all up and stick it in a bowl
  2. Salt and lime to taste
